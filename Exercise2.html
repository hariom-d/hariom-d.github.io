Train Your Brain

The purpose of this exercise is to help you recall without any cue what you learned today/last exercise.
This is a very powerful technique as recalling the expressions you already know is a big challenge when you speak.

  • Exercise
  • Recall

Fill in the blank with appropriate words/expressions:

put together, right, substance, limit

  1. Could you please ..... the high level design .......
  2. Is there any ..... to this allegation?
  3. We have to .... the wrongs done by previous government.
  4. We need to .... the people in the meeting to 5.

Can you recall the expressions you learned in the last exercise?
