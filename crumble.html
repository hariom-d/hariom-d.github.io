Crumble/crumbly Student/Adv to fall apart
Popularity : Medium
Context: Commonly spoken with wall/building/relationship/emotions/plans/finances etc..
How to memorize : cookie is crumbled

Examples...
  1. This house is very old. All the plaster is crumbling.
  2. The relationship between Sam and Sally is crumbling.
  3. She crumbled after the death of her father.
  4. The cookie was very crumbly.
