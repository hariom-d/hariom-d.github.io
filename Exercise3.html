Train your brain

The purpose of this exercise is to help you recall without any cue what you learned since a specific time in the past(1/3/7 days).
This is a very powerful technique as recalling the expressions you already know is a big challenge when you speak.
Can you recall the expressions you learned in the last exercise?

This will be a bulk verify functionality. Differences will be highlighted.

It is highly recommended that users complete this exercise everyday. It is generally accepted that you should recall the learned material first time within 3 days and the second time after a span of a week.
You can do mental recall or write on a piece of paper also. We suggest writing on a piece of paper:)

One more technique: Watch a video that you have already seen before, but this time with sound and subtitles switched off.
Recall the expressions mentally as the video is being played.
It is generally said that we recall well when we are put in the same situation as we had in the past. Transport yourself back in the past:)
